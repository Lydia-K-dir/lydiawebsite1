About Me

Lydia is a Data Analyst, with a strong interest in transforming raw data into meaningful insights for decision-making.

She holds a Bachelor's degree in Nutition and Food Science from the University of Ghana, Accra (LEGON)

Lydia has a passion for turnng raw data to actionable insights.She has worked on several projects that required her to collect, clean, and analyze data using various tools and techniques. Her expertise includes data visualization, statistical analysis, and predictive modeling. She is proficient in using tools such as Power BI, SQL, and Python to extract insights from complex datasets.

In addition to her technical skills, Lydia is a strong communicator and collaborator. She has experience working with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements and deliver data-driven solutions. Lydia is always eager to learn and stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in the field of data analytics. She is committed to continuous improvement and is always looking for ways to enhance her skills and knowledge.
